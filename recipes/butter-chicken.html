Butter Chicken

Description

Ingredients:

Marination:

  • 1 lb. chicken
  • 3/4 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp kasuri methi
  • 1/ tsp turmeric
  • 3/4 tsp garam masala
  • 3/4 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt

Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 stick cinnamon
  • 2 pods green cardamom
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 green chilis
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 17 cashew nuts
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tbsp kasuri methi
  • 1/4 tbsp sugar
  • salt to taste
  • 80-100 ml heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp cilantro

Steps:

  1. First, marinate chicken with lemon juice, chili powder and salt for 20 minutes.
  2. Then marinate again with yogurt, kasuri methi, oil, turmeric, ginger garlic paste and garam masala.
  3. Cover and rest in refridgerator for 12-48 hours.
  4. Cook chicken in oven (240 C) or on the stove for 20-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  5. To make the gravy, first add cashews and tomatoes to a blender jar. Make a smooth puree.
  6. Next, put butter in a pan and heat it. Then saute the cinnamon, cloves and cardamom for a minute.
  7. Add ginger garlic paste and green chilis, then fry until fragrant.
  8. Next, pour in the tomato cashew puree.
  9. Add red chili powder, sugar and salt.
  10. Mix and cook until the tomato puree thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  11. Pour in 1 cup of water. The amount of water used can be changed to reach the desired consistency.
  12. Bring to a boil and simmer for 3-5 minutes.
  13. Add the chicken and simmer for about 5 minutes.
  14. Add garam masala and kasuri methi.
  15. Stir and simmer for 2-3 minutes, then pour cold heavy cream and turn the stove off.
  16. Garnish the butter chicken with cilantro and extra cream.
  17. Serve with rice, naan or paratha.
